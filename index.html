Balance

Elara

Elara is designed to reward precise play. When she survives at low HP, she can become a powerhouse, but her kit made that state a bit too easy to maintain.

To better reinforce her risk-reward identity:

  • ▪ Increased Elara's Special cost to 5 Mana
  • ▪ Reduced her healing to 1 HP

We'll be keeping a close eye on Elara's performance and will make further adjustments if needed.
