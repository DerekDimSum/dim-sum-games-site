Dim Sum Games Pte. Ltd. (“Dim Sum Games,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile games, Fables & Tactics, Dim Sum Queens, Tiny Tactics GO!, and any related services (collectively, the “Services”).

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to improve your gaming experience and provide our Services effectively.

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

Account Information: If you create an account, we may collect your email, username, and profile details.

Customer Support: If you contact us, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email, and issue details.

User-Generated Content: Content you submit in the game, including chat messages and interactions.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Gameplay Data: Your progress, achievements, and in-game interactions.

Device Information: IP address, device model, operating system, and app version.

Usage Data: How you interact with the game, including session length and in-app purchases.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies, analytics, and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and improve the game.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

Social Media and Login Services: If you sign in using Google, Apple, or other third-party logins, we may receive your name, email, and profile picture from those services.

Ad and Analytics Partners: We may receive aggregated insights from ad networks or analytics providers, such as Google Analytics or Unity Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the games and their features.
  • Personalize gameplay and user experience.
  • Process in-app purchases and transactions.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, security issues, or policy violations.
  • Send important notifications, updates, or marketing, with consent where required.
  • Analyze game performance and optimize development.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share information with:

We take steps to ensure that third-party providers follow industry standards for data protection.
